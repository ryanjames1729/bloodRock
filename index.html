To use the table above, click on an aid station in real time when your runner goes through that aid station. Then, the approximate times for the next aid stations will be populated to plan when to meet your runner. The 2 columns are for Constant Pace for a runner that runs an even pace throughout the race and Adjusted Pace for a runner that will take more time as the race goes on due to changing shoes, eating, fatigue, etc.

For the aid stations with hard cut-offs, the times will turn red if the predicted times are not under the cut-off times.
